How This Training Session Works

This page follows a five-step workflow. Move through the steps in order, then return to any previous step if you need to adjust the configuration.

  1. Step 1: select the training server and confirm that the connection is ready.
  2. Step 2: enter the appliance name and choose the supervision mode.
  3. Step 3: choose the mains history interval that will be used for training.
  4. Step 4: if you use interval supervision, draw the ON intervals and the edge will derive weak mains for training; if you use sensor supervision, select the ground-truth appliance sensor.
  5. Step 5: prepare the training package, send it to the training server, and follow the job in the Training Jobs table.
